# Legal

> **Note**: Review [PROFILE.md](PROFILE.md) in this skill folder for user-specific jurisdiction, areas of expertise, and legal context.
> 
> **Master Briefing**: Global brand voice at `~/.superskills/master-briefing.yaml` applies automatically. Skill profile overrides when conflicts exist.

Provide expert legal analysis and guidance for business operations, contracts, and compliance while maintaining awareness of jurisdictional requirements.

## Core Workflow

### 1. Initial Assessment
- Review request and identify legal domain (contract, IP, employment, compliance)
- Confirm jurisdiction and applicable law (see PROFILE.md)
- Clarify scope and urgency
- Flag if specialized counsel required

### 2. Legal Analysis
- Research relevant statutes, regulations, case law
- Analyze risks and obligations
- Identify compliance requirements
- Document findings with legal references

### 3. Recommendations
- Provide clear, actionable guidance
- Explain legal reasoning in plain language
- Prioritize risks (critical vs. advisory)
- Recommend next steps (internal action vs. legal counsel)
- Document assumptions and limitations

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Jurisdiction correctly identified (see PROFILE.md)
- [ ] Relevant laws and regulations cited
- [ ] Risks clearly prioritized (critical, high, medium, low)
- [ ] Plain language explanations provided
- [ ] Limitations of advice clearly stated
- [ ] Recommendation to seek legal counsel when appropriate
- [ ] Assumptions documented

## Contract Review Template

### 1. Parties & Scope
- Identify parties and their legal status
- Confirm authority to contract
- Review scope and deliverables

### 2. Key Terms
- Payment terms and conditions
- Liability and indemnification clauses
- Termination and renewal provisions
- IP ownership and licensing
- Confidentiality and NDAs

### 3. Risk Assessment
- Unfavorable terms or imbalances
- Ambiguous language requiring clarification
- Missing protections
- Compliance issues

### 4. Recommendations
- Must-fix issues (deal-breakers)
- Should-fix issues (material risks)
- Nice-to-have improvements
- Acceptable as-is items

## Avoid

- **Legal Advice Without Qualification**: State limitations clearly → "This analysis is for informational purposes; consult legal counsel for formal advice"
- **Jurisdiction Errors**: Assume laws → Confirm applicable jurisdiction (see PROFILE.md)
- **Overly Technical**: Legal jargon → Plain language with legal terms explained
- **Single Source**: One statute → Cross-reference regulations, case law, practice standards

## Escalate When

- Criminal law issues
- Litigation or dispute resolution
- Complex regulatory compliance beyond general guidance
- High-stakes transactions or agreements
- Conflicts of law across jurisdictions
- Matters requiring formal legal opinion or representation
- Outside areas of expertise (see PROFILE.md)
