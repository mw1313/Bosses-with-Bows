# Trendwatcher

> **Note**: Review [PROFILE.md](PROFILE.md) in this skill folder for user-specific industries, sources, and monitoring priorities.
> 
> **Master Briefing**: Global brand voice at `~/.superskills/master-briefing.yaml` applies automatically. Skill profile overrides when conflicts exist.

Systematically monitor, analyze, and report on trends to identify opportunities, threats, and strategic insights.

## Core Workflow

### 1. Signal Collection
- Monitor designated sources and channels
- Capture weak signals and emerging patterns
- Track competitor moves and announcements
- Gather data from multiple perspectives

### 2. Pattern Analysis
- Identify recurring themes and patterns
- Distinguish signal from noise
- Assess trend strength and momentum
- Connect dots across disparate signals

### 3. Impact Assessment
- Evaluate relevance to business/domain
- Assess potential opportunities and threats
- Estimate timeline and probability
- Prioritize by strategic importance

### 4. Reporting & Recommendations
- Synthesize findings into clear insights
- Provide actionable recommendations
- Suggest monitoring priorities
- Share timely alerts and updates

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Multiple sources consulted for validation
- [ ] Trends distinguished from fads
- [ ] Business relevance clearly articulated
- [ ] Opportunities and threats identified
- [ ] Timeline and probability estimated
- [ ] Actionable recommendations provided
- [ ] Sources cited and credible
- [ ] Regular cadence maintained

## Trend Evaluation Criteria

**Strength Assessment:**
- **Weak Signal**: Isolated mentions, unverified, speculative
- **Emerging**: Multiple independent sources, early adoption
- **Strong**: Widespread discussion, proven use cases, investment
- **Mainstream**: Broad adoption, established players, commoditizing

**Opportunity/Threat Matrix:**
- **High Impact + High Probability**: Immediate action required
- **High Impact + Low Probability**: Monitor closely, prepare contingency
- **Low Impact + High Probability**: Minor adjustments
- **Low Impact + Low Probability**: Awareness only

## Avoid

- **Single Source**: One article → Validate across multiple sources
- **Hype Chasing**: Every new thing → Filter for relevance and substance
- **No Context**: Raw trends → Explain business implications
- **Data Dump**: List everything → Synthesize and prioritize
- **No Cadence**: Sporadic monitoring → Regular, systematic tracking
- **Ignoring Contradictions**: Cherry-pick signals → Include opposing views

## Escalate When

- Critical trend requires immediate strategic response
- Major competitor move threatens position
- Opportunity window closing rapidly
- Regulatory/policy changes impact business
- Technology disruption accelerating
- Need for specialized domain expertise
