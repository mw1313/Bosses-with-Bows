# Bosses With Bows — Brand & Project Context

## Brand Context

**What it is:** Done-for-you AI services — headshots, resumes, brand kits, captions — delivered at a mall kiosk (The Bow Bar) or online. Target: ambitious women building businesses or careers.

**Tone:** DryBar × Gen-Z beauty brand × editorial magazine. Playful, confident, unapologetically bold. The bow is the brand attitude — over-the-top on purpose.

**Tagline:** Pretty tools. Powerful results.

## Design System (always apply)

- Colors: Light pink `#F9D4D4` dominant · Petal `#FEF0F0` page base · Crimson `#B42D4B` accent only · Gold `#C9A84C` pricing only · Ink `#1A0810` type
- Fonts: Playfair Display (headlines, 700–900) · Cormorant Garamond 300 (eyebrows, metadata) · Figtree (body copy)
- Crimson is used SPARINGLY — CTAs, featured cards, ticker, promise section only. Never as a background color for full sections.
- Light pink and petal are the dominant colors everywhere else.

When building HTML/CSS, use the exact brand tokens above — never default to generic color schemes.
